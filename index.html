Why SSC Rejects Photos (And How This Fixes It)

Every year, thousands of forms are rejected on portals like SSC, UPSC, and IBPS due to improper photo uploads. Our tool automatically prevents the three most common errors:

  • Missing DOP: SSC strictly requires a white strip at the bottom with your Name and Date of Photo (DOP). This tool generates it with mathematically perfect proportions.
  • Size Limits (20KB - 50KB): Manual compression often drops the file size too low (causing blur) or leaves it too high (causing rejection). We use a binary search algorithm to hit exactly within the limit.
  • Aspect Ratio: Selfies or uncropped photos are rejected. We force a 3.5x4.5 ratio before adding the text panel.
