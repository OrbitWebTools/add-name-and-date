Why Does SSC Reject My Photo? — 3 Most Common Reasons

SSC exam photo rejection happens due to these specific errors. Our tool fixes all three automatically:

  • Missing Name and DOP Strip: SSC explicitly requires your full name and Date of Photo written at the bottom in black text on a white background strip. This tool adds it automatically with the correct font size and contrast.
  • File Size Outside 20KB–50KB: Photos above 50KB are rejected outright by the SSC portal. Photos below 20KB appear too blurry. Our compression algorithm hits exactly within range every time.
  • Wrong Aspect Ratio: Your selfie or studio photo may be square or landscape. The tool auto-crops it to the correct 3.5cm × 4.5cm portrait ratio before adding the text strip.
